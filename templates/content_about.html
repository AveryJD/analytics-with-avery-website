About Analytics by Avery

Analytics by Avery is my personal online platform where I showcase my work in sports analytics, with a strong focus on hockey. It's a space where I combine my technical skills and passion for sports to share projects, visualizations, and ideas that dig deeper into the numbers behind the game.

On this site, you can find my hockey analytics player card project, a model built using NHL player data to evaluate and rank players based on various performance attributes. I also write a blog where I explore research, trends, and statistical insights across the world of sports. My goal is to make advanced analytics more engaging and accessible to fans, analysts, and aspiring data scientists alike.

Whether you're here to browse my latest hockey data projects or follow my journey in the world of sports analytics, I hope you find something interesting, informative, or just fun to dive into.
